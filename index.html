About

Teamfight Tactics is a strategic free-for-all game of attrition featuring iconic champions from League of Legends. Your goal? Grow your team and protect your board to become the last player standing.

Game Info

Teamfight Tactics is an autobattler: a round-based PvP game where you’ll draft a team that battles automatically on your behalf. If you've never played an autobattler before, don't worry! TFT borrows from many of the concepts you know and love about other strategy games, like drafting from a shared pool of resources, team building, economy management, and positioning.

Game Info

Individuals face off in 1-on-1 matches until only one player remains. You win by drafting the best team of champions from a shared pool, equipping them with powerful items, managing your gold income to buy out the competition, and positioning your team in winning battle formations. There are unlimited winning strategies, so you’ll have to adapt to different situations in every game to come out on top.

Game Info

Every game of TFT takes place in the Convergence, and centers around a specific theme and story called a set. Every few months TFT introduces you to a new universe with new champions, traits, and characters to play. In terms of gameplay, new sets also come with a new Ranked ladder to climb, new team compositions, and new item possibilities.

Game Info

In the current set, Teamfight Tactics: Reckoning, the Black Mist has made its way to the Convergence, corrupting everything in its path and schewing the balance between chaos and order. As the forces clash in the ultimate battle for balance, players must choose whether to stand with the guardians of order, or revel with the agents of chaos

RecentSets

Set 9.5

Set About

Teamfight Tactics Set 9.5 is Horizonbound-themed and features new traits, champions, and items. The new traits are Ixtal, Bilgewater, and Vanquisher. Ixtal champions create elemental hexes that give different bonuses to nearby champions. Bilgewater champions mark enemies with their attacks and abilities, which deals damage after 2 seconds. Vanquisher champions deal bonus critical strike damage to enemies with higher health. Set 9.5 is shaping up to be an exciting new set with a lot of new and creative content.

Set 9

Set About

Set 9 features new champions, traits, items, and mechanics, including Region Portals and Legends. Players can vote for a region at the start of the game for a global buff, and upgrade powerful champions called Legends. Some strategies to explore include building a team around a specific trait or building a well-rounded team that adapts to different situations. Be flexible, aware of your team's strengths and weaknesses, and don't be afraid to roll. Have fun!

Set 8.5

Set About

Set 8.5 of Teamfight Tactics, also known as Glitched Out, is a mid-set update that features new Hero Augments, an overhauled item system, and a new roster of champions and items. Hero Augments are powerful modifiers that can be applied to champions, giving them new abilities or boosting their stats. Items are now dropped from a variety of different sources, including Hero Augments, Orbs, and the Carousel. Some of the new champions include Vex, Jayce, and Orianna. Some of the new items include Infinity Force, Hextech Gunblade, and Jeweled Gauntlet. Set 8.5 is a major update for Teamfight Tactics, and it brings a lot of new and exciting content to the game.
